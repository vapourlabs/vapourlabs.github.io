Open Source

The DCS modding community is responsible for the many impressive mods available today.However, many good ones are closed-sourced. This stops others from wanting to either understand how something works, or modify it for improvement.Vapour Labs believes in contributing to OSS. Everything created by us is for free, by the community and for the community.

Collaboration

We collaborate and work with various partners in the DCS modding community to achieve incredible feats.Our latest work focuses on improving 3D modelling for Blender users with modeldrop, written in Python.We also plan to release boilerplate for creating any DCS mod structure later on down the road.

  • Who We Are

Vapour Labs is an international team of artists and software engineers.We are leaders instead of followers, creating projects suitable for large audiences such as the MiG-25RBT project.We have knowledgeable skills ranging in systems engineering, computational fluid dynamics analysis, hard-surface modelling and other fields.We cater to using general-purpose programming languages such as C, C++ and Zig. We use bleeding edge technology to write future-proof software.It is our biggest goal at Vapour Labs, to achieve software that is free and open to others contributing. We value allowing ourselves to serve as a vendor in the DCS community, as well as accepting other vendors to co-exist with our work. Nobody has ever said "give us software that we can't edit when we need to."
